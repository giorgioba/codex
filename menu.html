Pairing Philosophy

Curated by botanists, composed by sommeliers

Every pairing is an exploration of terroir across time. Our sommeliers collaborate with perfumers to craft aromatic arcs that mirror the plating choreography.

Choose between the Phosphorescent Flight, highlighting biodynamic vineyards aligned with lunar cycles, or the Zenith Non-Alcoholic Sequence featuring clarified teas, smoked tonics, and sparkling kefir.

Enhance the evening with a Resonance Lounge Prelude, where you can preview botanicals through scent libraries and bespoke audio vignettes that attune your senses before dinner.
