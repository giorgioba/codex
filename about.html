Our Story

The botanists, composers, and dreamers of Lúmina

We are a collective of culinary artists, perfumers, sound designers, and horticulturists united by a belief: dining can become a living artwork.

Rooted in Reverence

Founder and chef Marisol Vega grew up in the mountains of Oaxaca, where she learned to read seasons through the whisper of leaves. After leading kitchens in Barcelona and Kyoto, she returned to Canada to build a restaurant that honors every farmer, forager, and pollinator.

The Collective

Lúmina assembles an interdisciplinary team: perfumer Jules Osei, synesthetic composer Aya Lian, botanist duo Harper & Noor, and sustainability futurist Micah Thorn. Together, they craft evenings that pulse with care for the planet.

Regenerative Practices

We partner with regenerative farms across Ontario, grow rare botanicals in our rooftop greenhouse, and offset our footprint through rewilding collaborations with Indigenous land stewards.

Meet the creators

Botanists Harper & Noor

Living Ecosystems

We treat every ingredient as part of a living story, respecting its origin and future impact.
