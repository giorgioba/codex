Seasonal Feature

The Equinox Reverie Tasting

A nine-movement tasting that mirrors the shifting light of spring. Each course unveils a luminous pairing of rare botanicals and culinary innovation.

Verdant Prelude

Spherical chilled pea consommé, suspended herb oils, elderflower mist, and a sonic landscape tuned to 528Hz.

Northern Aurora

Charred cedar-smoked scallops with spruce tip granité beneath responsive fiber optics mimicking the aurora borealis.

Moonlit Harvest

Slow-braised rabbit, luminous black garlic lacquer, fermented parsnip silk, and field recordings of nocturnal pollinators.

Starlit Finale

Liquid nitrogen jasmine cloud, cocoa nib praline, and edible bioluminescent pearls that glow softly at the table.
