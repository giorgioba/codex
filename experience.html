Immersive Chapters

A constellation of curated experiences

Every room at Lúmina is designed to heighten a sense—taste, scent, sound, light, and touch—so that the evening unfolds as a synesthetic narrative.

The Resonance Lounge

Prelude to your tasting journey with cocktails tuned to the frequencies of seasonal botanicals.

  • Frequency Pairings

    A sound alchemist composes micro-symphonies that alter as you sip, aligning with the botanicals in your glass.

  • Scent Library

    Curated aromatic archives invite you to identify the botanical notes that will appear in your meal.

  • Chromatic Seating

    Choose the hue that resonates with your mood and let the lighting respond in real time.

The Chef's Observatory

An intimate ten-seat counter where Chef Marisol Vega orchestrates rare tasting movements.

Celestial Pairings

Courses sync with a projected skyscape of the evening sky, mapping constellations to ingredients.

Interactive Plating

Guests are invited to finish their courses with aromatic mists and edible petals guided by the chef.

Chef's Discourse

Discover the stories behind each course as Chef Vega shares insights into indigenous growers and foragers.

The Whispering Conservatory

A glasshouse sanctuary where botanists lead micro-workshops before and after dinner.

Botanical Briefing

Meet our resident botanists to learn how every ingredient is cultivated sustainably.

Aroma Cartography

Participate in scent-mapping exercises that reveal the aromatic DNA of each course.

Nocturnal Finale

Close the evening surrounded by rare night-blooming flora as ambient composers perform live.

The Synesthetic Salon

A private salon for bespoke celebrations, complete with customizable light sculptures and edible art installations.

Collaborate with our creative technologists to design a tasting narrative that reflects your story. From sculptural centerpieces crafted with aeroponic herbs to responsive projection mapping, the Synesthetic Salon invites you to imprint your dreams onto the evening.
